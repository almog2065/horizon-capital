# Investment Policy

§1 Universe. **Core:** US-listed equities only, market cap above 5 billion USD.
**Satellite (optional, policy §8):** spot crypto (BTC, ETH via CoinGecko) and
commodity exposure via listed ETFs (GLD, USO, SLV, DBC) — max 10% NAV Digital
Assets aggregate, 5% per crypto name; Commodities sector subject to 25% cap.
Excluded sectors: tobacco, gambling, defense primes, predatory lending.
Excluded statuses: under SEC investigation, late filers, going-concern doubts.
Crypto has no SEC filing requirement; commodity proxies follow ETF liquidity rules.

§2 Position sizing. New position entry size 3% to 5% of NAV. Max single
position 8% of NAV. Max sector exposure 25% of NAV. Cash floor 5% of NAV.
Max concurrent open plans 25.

§3 Holding discipline. Minimum intended holding period 12 months. No re-entry
within 30 days of sell. Watchlist seasoning minimum 4 weeks before plan.

§4 Entry criteria. Plan eligible only if all are true:
fundamental thesis with at least 3 supporting points from independent sources;
current valuation within target range; no earnings within 14 days;
no SEC investigation; post-entry sector exposure under 25%;
both Technical Analyst and Fundamental Analyst have produced reads.

§5 Exit triggers. Move plan to review status (NOT auto-exit) on any of:
thesis violation, valuation overshoot, CEO or CFO departure, restatement,
guidance cut over 15%, position grown beyond 8% NAV. Exits are NOT triggered
by price drops alone.

§6 Rebalancing cadence. Quarterly full re-underwriting. Monthly drift check.
Weekly open plans review. Daily integrity check only.

§7 Information discipline. No trade on news older than 48 hours. No new
positions in last 30 min of session. Earnings blackout 7 days before, 24
hours after. All evidence timestamped; agents filter to as_of.
